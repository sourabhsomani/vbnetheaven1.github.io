Asha Jyot Foundation Samiti

Drug De-Addiction , counseling & Rehabilitation Center..

The Origin

For the  purpose to prevent  Drug Abuse in the Society, Asha Jyot Foundation Samiti was formed & got registered on 2nd April 2013.Our NGO got regular license for De-addiction, Counselling & Rehabilitation on 25-06-2015 from Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment, Government of India .On the occasion of Swarna Jyanti NGO,s Sammelan 18th August 2017 our center got awarded with the certificate of appreciation by Ministery of Social Justice & empowerment Haryana.

Objectives

The followings are the main objectives of the Centre:-

To motivate drug abusers for de- addiction

To provide treatment to the admitted drug addicts.

To provide Psycho-social services for Rehabilitation of drug abusers.

To create awareness among the people regarding the impact of drug abuses by organizing various programs such as Song & Drama/Skits, Seminar, Camps, Rallies, Exhibitions, Educational Competitions etc.

Our Gallery

Follow Up Activities

Alcoholic Anonymous & Narcotic Anonymous Meetings
Yoga and meditation classes are mandatory for all patients.

We arrange the Alcoholic Anonymous & Narcotic Anonymous Meetings time to time at the Centre.

All discharged recovered persons and other drug users can also attend these meetings

After treatment, follow-up of all discharged drug free persons is done by our Social Workers and staff members.

Other Activities

The Centre organizes following awareness generation activities for drug abuse prevention.

De-addiction awareness camps.

Seminars, rallies, exhibitions, song & drama shows, glimpses etc

Training programs, community level meetings, conventions and other programmes.

Coordination with Health Department, other Government Departments, NGOs and Social Activists for controlling the problem of Drug Abuse.

Admission Rules

The drug addicts are admitted only for indoor treatment in the Centre from 9.00 pM to 7.00 PM on Monday to Saturday if the drug user is under conscious mind & motivated for De- Addiction treatment.

The drug addict will have to stay up to minimum one month (recommended) by the centre for treatment and rehabilitation.

Patients guardians need to give their approval also.

Rules and Regulation

The following rules and regulations of the Centre are applicable for all Drug Addicts.

To follow the daily time table & discipline of center.

To live in the Centre without the family members.

Once patient got mentally & physically fit then he has to do daily works at his own.

To participate in all type of group activities organized by the Centre
